# Privacy Policy for HALO – Anonymous Safe Zone

Effective Date: [Today’s Date]

## 1. Information We Collect
HALO is designed to be anonymous by default. We do not require users to provide names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information to use the app.

We may collect:
- Anonymous user-generated content (confessions/posts)
- Basic device information (device type, OS version) for app stability
- Anonymous usage data for improving app performance

## 2. How We Use Information
Collected information is used only to:
- Provide and maintain the app
- Improve safety, moderation, and performance
- Prevent abuse and violations of our community rules

We do not sell or rent user data.

## 3. Anonymity
Posts and interactions in HALO are anonymous. We do not publicly display or share personal identifiers.

## 4. Content Moderation
Content may be automatically or manually reviewed to ensure safety and compliance with our policies. Content that violates rules may be removed.

## 5. Third-Party Services
HALO may use trusted third-party services (such as analytics or crash reporting tools) that collect limited anonymous data solely for app functionality.

## 6. Data Security
We use reasonable security measures to protect data from unauthorized access or misuse.

## 7. Children’s Privacy
HALO is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
