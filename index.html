Privacy Policy for HALO – Anonymous Safe Zone

Effective Date: January 2026

HALO – Anonymous Safe Zone (“HALO”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

HALO is designed to be anonymous by default. Users are not required to provide names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.

We may collect the following limited data:

  • Anonymous user-generated content (confessions or posts)
  • Basic device information (device type, operating system version)
  • Anonymous usage data for performance and stability improvements

2. How We Use Information

The collected information is used only to:

  • Provide and maintain the application
  • Improve safety, moderation, and app performance
  • Prevent abuse and violations of community rules

HALO does not sell, rent, or trade user data.

3. Anonymity

All posts and interactions within HALO are anonymous. We do not publicly display or share personal identifiers.

4. Content Moderation

Content may be automatically or manually reviewed to ensure safety and policy compliance. Content that violates platform rules may be removed.

5. Third-Party Services

HALO may use trusted third-party services (such as analytics or crash reporting tools) that collect limited anonymous data strictly for app functionality and reliability.

6. Data Security

Reasonable security measures are used to protect data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

7. Children’s Privacy

HALO is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
